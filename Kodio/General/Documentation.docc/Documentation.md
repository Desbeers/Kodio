# ``Kodio``

Play your own music

## Overview

Kodio is a Remote Control for macOS and iPadOS to play your music with [Kodi](https://kodi.tv).

It is using my [SwiftlyKodiApi](https://github.com/Desbeers/SwiftlyKodiAPI) framework for the heavy lifting.
